Given an array, you have to print the maximum XOR operation of two numbers in the given array.

Input Decsription:
Numbers in array

Output Description:
Maximum XOR of any two numbers in the array

Sample input:
3 10 5 25 2 8

Sample output:
28

Explanation:
5^28 gives 28 which is maximum than any other XOR of other numbers.

Sample input:
77 3 4 34 19

Sample output:
111

Explanation:
77 ^ 34 gives 111.
